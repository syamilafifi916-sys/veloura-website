About Us

Where Linen Meets Elegance

VELOURA is a contemporary modest fashion brand specializing in premium 100% pure linen apparel for the modern Malaysian woman.

Proudly tailored by local hands in Malaysia, each piece is crafted from premium high-density pure linen with rich visible slub texture for confident opacity and breathable comfort.

Thoughtfully designed, locally made and released in limited quantities. Veloura celebrates women of different shapes and sizes through inclusive sizing from XS to 4XL.
